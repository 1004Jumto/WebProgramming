1. Pinterest

Pinterest(meaning Pin+interest) is a social network service in which users post images they want to scrap and share them with others. Find and save ideas to try, such as recipes, house decorating ideas, and inspiring styles.

2. Artstation

The ArtStation provides an awesome browsing and discovery experience, enabling you to view thousands of artworks by the world's best artists. The quality of the work is generally very high because it has a large number of graphic artists and aspiring artists working in game companies and is used as a portfolio.

3. ConceptArtWorld

Concept art is a form of illustration that was conceived before the final product was completed, with the design and atmosphere used in movies, video games, animation, and manga. Concept Art World features concept artists, illustrators and designers.

4. NoteFolio

Notefolio is a space where artists and designers scattered around gather in one place to reveal and talk about their work. It has detailed categories within the site, such as graphic design, video/motion graphic, industrial design, and illustration, so you can collect the design types you want.
