1. PANTONE

Pantone meaning("all colors") developed the first color matching system. The Pantone Color Matching Syste(PMS) is largely a standardized color reproduction system. It is helpful to select trendy Color.

2. COLRD

Be inspired by the most popular colors, palettes, gradients, and patterns in their database. Choosing the perfect color scheme is an important part of any website or app design, they assembled a helpful list of othe best color palette generators.

3. Paletton

Paletton is not just a professional's tool, it was designed to help all sorts of color projects, from professional work. If you choose your color, they provide the color design for blog, websites, presentations, etc.

4. ColorMatch 5K

THis utility will help you select a matching 6-color palette for your work. Define a single color that you like. Matching colors will be calculated.

5. UIGradients

A handpicked collection of beautiful color gradients for designers and developers. It applies a color and transparency gradient to the UI elements rendered by the parent GuiObject.
