See and feel fashion trends,
Refer to your Art

1. LOOKBOOK

A lookbook is a collection of photographs compiled to show off a model, photographer, style, stylist, or clothing line. The website is free and allows everyone to join. It allows users to share their looks and activity in Facebook, Twitter. It has been discussed in major fashion and media outlets.

2. STREETPEEPER

Street Peeper is a global street fashion photo site, featuring the best street style from cities like New York, Berlin, Paris, London, Tokyo, and more.

3. Fashion-Era

Fashion-Era analysis the change of social, history and lifestyle to the current and historical fashion trends. It is characterized by providing detailed categories of fashion according to social culture by era.

4. SketchDaily References

SketchDaily is a free online educational tool designed to help you improve your sketching and drawing skills. The good thing is that you can set the clocking time, model, gender, and composition of the model. It is not limited to humans, but also provides scenery and animal croquis.

5. Kitasite

This website is a site that provides croquis focused on cartoon drawing. Various poses are provided for each body, and brief descriptions are available for each body part. In addition, categories are divided according to levels, so you can raise the level step by step. You can see many people's cartoon character styles as a bonus.
