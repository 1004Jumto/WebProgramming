1. Proko

Proko is a resource for artists to get fun and informative art instruction videos which it's Youtube with approximately 2.54 million subscribers. He runs a YouTube channel, but also a paid course website. If you want to take a lecture that is more detailed and fits your level, paying is a worthwhile course.

2. Pene Menn

Pene Menn means "good person" in Norwegian. Also she runs an academy called "Fene Art School". In particular, the background-related courses are famous and have great skills. She is also a concept artist.

3. IRADUKAI

It is not a painting site, but it is a Japanese site that is very helpful when coloring. It's old, but the method of coloring is detailed, so if you copy it, you can improve your coloring skills. And it will be useful to take a course on the program you use at least once as it teaches you how to color using various software.

4. Starland Painting

It is a YouTube course channel recommended for beginners who study painting on their own. In the picture, you can strengthen the basic skills of practice, watercolor, and brushwork. It also guides beginners in detail from the beginning of the painting to the comparison of materials, paper, and water audits.

5. Jay Lee Painting

It is a lecture channel on watercolor painting using various watercolor techniques and living tools. The contents of the course are subdivided from beginner to advanced, and it is easy to follow because they kindly show it from beginning to end. In addition to brushes, you can have more fun because you use tools.
